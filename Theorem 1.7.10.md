If $\mathbf{f}$ is [[derivative|differentiable]] at $\mathbf{a}$, then all [[partial derivative]]s of $\mathbf{f}$ at $\mathbf{a}$ exist, and the [[matrix]] representing $[\mathbf{Df}(\mathbf{a})]$ is [[jacobian matrix]] $\mathbf{Jf}(\mathbf{a})]$.
