Let $U$ be an [[open set|open]] subset of $\mathbb{R}^n$ and $f:U\to\mathbb{R}$ a function. The [[partial derivative]] of $f$ with respect to the $i$th variable, and evaluated at $\mathbf{a}$, is the limit$$D_if(\mathbf{a}):=\lim_{h\to 0}\frac{1}{h}(f\begin{pmatrix}a_1 \\ \vdots \\ a_i+h \\ \vdots \\ a_n\end{pmatrix}-f\begin{pmatrix}a_1 \\ \vdots \\ a_i \\ \vdots \\ a_n\end{pmatrix}),$$if the limit exists, of course.

Notations
$$\frac{\partial f}{\partial{x_i}} \equiv D_if$$
